Engineering Geospatial Technologies for the Future

Hardware

GeoMANiK Labs offers proprietary hardware designed for seamless, reliable, and secure GIS applications. Our IP catalogue includes long-range data transmission hardware that works well with drone technologies, Global Navigation Satellite Systems with high spatial resolution and positioning accuracy, Remote Sensing Platforms, and NetKinnect devices for Cloud-Management Applications. To meet the demands of an ever-evolving global market for GIS applications, GeoMANiK Labs focuses on developing highly modular systems that add functionality to existing GIS technologies, with emphasis on designing infrastructure that are not only robust, but offer security, scalability, flexibility, and ease of integration into existing systems.

Software

At GeoMANiK Labs, our approach is defined by high integration of both software and hardware, creating solutions that seamlessly complement your existing GIS infrastructure. Our proprietary Image Analysis and Visualization Software empowers users to not only interpret aerial and satellite imagery but also to construct interactive maps that bring complex data to life. We specialize in leveraging LiDAR for data processing to create highly accurate Digital Elevation Models, which are essential for environmental modeling and urban planning. Furthermore, our systems provide realtime asset tracking over maps, offering dynamic, up-to-the-minute insights into your operations directly within our platform.

Applications

Our diverse portfolio ensures a centralized and highly integrated ecosystem for companies looking to fully utilize and leverage geospatial information systems and technologies. We offer solutions for a wide range of applications including; Cartography, Land Surveying, Urban Planning, Geospatial Intelligence and Analytics, Asset Tracking, Critical Infrastructure Monitoring, Agriculture, and general GIS. This comprehensive suite of tools is designed to streamline operations and provide actionable insights, helping businesses across various sectors to make data-driven decisions and optimize their use of geographic data.
